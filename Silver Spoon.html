About The Song

"Silver Spoon" (뱁새; also known as "Baepsae" or "Crow-Tit") is a song by BTS. It was released on November 30, 2015, and appears as the sixth track for their fourth mini-album The Most Beautiful Moment in Life Pt.2 and as the eighth track of the CD 1 in their first compilation album The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Young Forever. This song has a Japanese version featured in their second Japanese studio album Youth.
