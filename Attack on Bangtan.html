About The Song

"Attack on Bangtan" (진격의 방탄; also known as "The Rise of Bangtan") is a song by BTS. It was released on September 11, 2013, and appears as the sixth track for their first mini album O!RUL8,2?. This song has a Japanese version featured in their first Japanese single "No More Dream" and in their first Japanese studio album Wake Up. A remixed version was released on November 19, 2014 and serves as the second track from their third Japanese single "Danger".
