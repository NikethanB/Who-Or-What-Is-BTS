About The Song

BTS Cypher Pt.3: Killer" is a song by RM, Suga and J-Hope (also known as the rap-line) of BTS, featuring Supreme Boi. The song was released on August 20, 2014. It was released on August 20, 2014 and appears as the seventh track for their first studio album Dark & Wild.
