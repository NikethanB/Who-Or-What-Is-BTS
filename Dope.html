About The Song

"Jjeoreo" is a song recorded by BTS. It can be found as track #5 on their third mini-album Hwayang Yeonhwa pt. 1. On international listings and digital platforms it is named "DOPE". A Japanese version of the song, titled "DOPE -Chou Yabee!- -Japanese Ver.-", was first released on their fifth Japanese single "I NEED U". The original track was used as theme song for their variety program Run BTS!.
